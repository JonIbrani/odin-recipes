Cheesecake Recipe

Description

To make a cheesecake, start by mixing 200g of crushed graham crackers (or digestive biscuits) with 100g of melted unsalted butter. Press this mixture firmly into the bottom of a springform pan to form the crust, and chill it in the fridge for about 10-15 minutes. For the filling, beat together 600g of softened cream cheese, 100g of granulated sugar, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract until smooth. Add 3 large eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Then, blend in 200g of sour cream and, if you want extra tang, add 1 tablespoon of lemon juice. Pour the filling over the chilled crust and bake at 325°F (163°C) for 50-60 minutes, until the edges are set but the center is slightly jiggly. Once done, turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake cool gradually for an hour. Afterward, refrigerate for at least 4 hours or, ideally, overnight. Serve with your favorite toppings, slice, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) graham crackers (or digestive biscuits), crushed
  • 100g (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, melted
  • 600g (3 cups) cream cheese, softened
  • 100g (1/2 cup) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 200g (1 cup) sour cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional, for extra tang)
